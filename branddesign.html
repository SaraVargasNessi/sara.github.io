LAGO Art Gallery

Light Art Gallery of Ontario (LAGO) is located at Harbourfront, Toronto. The acronym “LAGO” means lake in Spanish, which refers to the lake in front of the gallery, Lake Ontario. “Light” connects to the use of light in traditional and modern art. The gallery showcases local and international contemporary artists of all mediums year-round. The look and feel of the brand invite reflection and do not take away from the art.
